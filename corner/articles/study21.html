Actor-Critic

Actor-Critic 方法融合了 Value-based 和 Policy-based，其中 Actor 需要根据策略采取让 Critic 满意的动作，Critic 需要准确估计出 Actor 所采取的动作的价值。假设 Actor 为 $\pi_\theta$，Critic 为 $V_\phi$，其中 $\theta$ 和 $\phi$ 为参数，则基础的 Actor-Critic 算法就是在 Policy Gradient 上的延伸：

        \begin{algorithm}
            \caption{Actor-Critic Algorithm}
            \caption{Actor-Critic}
            \begin{algorithmic}
                \FOR{$t=1\to T$}
                    \STATE Sample action $a_t$ from $\pi_\theta(a_t\mid s_t)$ and execute it
                    \STATE Observe reward $r_t$ and next state $s_{t+1}$
                    
                    \STATE Estimate value $V_\phi(s_t)$ and $V_\phi(s_{t+1})$
                    \STATE Compute TD error: $\delta_t=r_t+\gamma V_\phi(s_{t+1})-V_\phi(s_t)$
                    \STATE $\phi\gets\phi+\alpha_\phi\delta_t\nabla_\phi V_\phi(s_t)$
                    
                    \STATE Compute policy gradient: $\nabla_\theta J(\theta) = \delta_t \nabla_\theta \log \pi_\theta(a_t | s_t)$
                    \STATE $\theta\gets\theta+\alpha_\theta\nabla_\theta J(\theta)$
                    
                    \STATE $s_t\gets s_{t+1}$
                \ENDFOR
            \end{algorithmic}
        \end{algorithm}
